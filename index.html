About Hotels in Orbit

Hospitality Beyond Earth

Hotels in Orbit is the definitive resource for anyone tracking the emerging space hospitality industry. We cover every planned orbital habitat, review concept designs, interview the companies building them, and keep a running tally of projected opening dates and pricing.

Why Space Hotels Matter

The race to build habitable space stations is not just about tourism. These facilities will serve as research platforms, manufacturing hubs, and the first permanent communities off Earth. Understanding them now means understanding where humanity lives next.
